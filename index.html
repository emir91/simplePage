How to make Bosnian coffe

In Bosnia and Herzegovina coffe isn't just drink, it is reason for conversation.

ingredients

  • Coffe
  • Water
  • Sugar (cube or cristal)

preparation

  1. Boil some water
  2. Put coffee grounds in a tiny metal pot
  3. Pour boiling water into tiny metal pot with coffee grounds
  4. Put tiny metal pot back on stove
  5. Scoop up the foam and put it in the bottom of your cup
